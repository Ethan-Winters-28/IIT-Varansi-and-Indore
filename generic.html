What are Generic Medicines?

Generic medicines are affordable alternatives to branded medicines. They contain the same active ingredients, have the same quality, strength, safety, and intended use as their branded counterparts, but are available at a fraction of the price.

Approved by regulatory authorities, these medicines make healthcare accessible and reduce the financial burden on patients without compromising treatment quality.

About Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP)

The Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) is a Government of India initiative aimed at providing high-quality generic medicines at affordable prices through dedicated outlets known as Jan Aushadhi Kendras. These kendras are established across India to ensure that every citizen has access to quality medicines without financial strain.
